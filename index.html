About

As a recent graduate in Computer Science Engineering with a specialization in Robotics and AI from Vellore Institute of Technology, I bring a blend of academic excellence and practical expertise. My internship at Smart Enterprise involved developing a 2-DOF robotics arm for diamond sorting, a project that won the Best Capstone Project Award at VIT Chennai. My research endeavors include innovative projects on structural batteries for drones and smart grid efficiencies, where I applied AI, IoT, and renewable energy technologies to real-world challenges.

In addition to my technical skills, I co-founded the NexSeed Club at VIT, where I led a team to support and accelerate student startups, fostering a dynamic entrepreneurial ecosystem. My diverse portfolio includes projects ranging from autonomous drone navigation to real-time speech translation systems, underscoring my commitment to technological innovation. My active involvement in clubs focused on humanoid robotics and electric vehicle technology further demonstrates my dedication to advancing practical applications of my skills.
